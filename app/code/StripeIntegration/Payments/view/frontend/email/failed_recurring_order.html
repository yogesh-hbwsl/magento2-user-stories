Important information:

You will need to pass the above error to your developer for resolution assistance. Once the problem has been resolved, you can create the subscription order by re-sending the webhook event. To re-send the event:

  • Navigate to {{var eventLink}}.
  • Scroll down to the Webhook attempts section.
  • If there is at least 1 successful delivery, do not re-send the event, as this will create 2 subscription orders for the same payment.
  • If there are no successful deliveries, click the Resend button to create the recurring subscription order.

Warning: You have 15 days to fix and resend the webhook event. After 15 days, you will not be able to re-send the event from the Stripe dashboard.
